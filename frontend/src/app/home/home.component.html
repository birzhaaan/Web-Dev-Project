What is Ko'rset?

Ko'rset is a platform that connects people in need with compassionate volunteers. Through real-time chat, help is always just a message away.

How It Works

📝 Request Help

Quickly submit a help request describing your situation.

👥 Get Matched

We'll connect you with an available volunteer in real-time.

💬 Start Chatting

Discuss your needs directly with a caring volunteer.

Why Ko'rset?

🙋 For Users

  • Easy and fast help requests
  • Talk to real people, not bots
  • 100% free support

🦸 For Volunteers

  • Make real impact
  • Flexible availability
  • Simple tools to assist people in need
